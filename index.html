This interactive presentation follows a martini glass structure to guide you through the critical indicators of climate change. We will start with an overview of global temperature anomalies, sea level rise, and CO2 emissions, and conclude with an engaging game that tests your knowledge about temperature variations worldwide. Together, these elements provide a comprehensive understanding of the urgency to address climate change.
